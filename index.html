About the Company

A Strong Network,
Built Over Decades

Basic Depth Sdn Bhd (BDSB) has been established since December 1996 with a clear aim — to concentrate on the emerging telecommunication market of Malaysia and serve its growing population of migrants and mobile users.

Over the years, BDSB has built a strong network throughout Malaysia, gathering extensive experience dealing with many well-known brands and service providers in the VOIP sector. Our portfolio has grown to become one of the most trusted choices among our valued customers.

We use the latest technologies and innovative solutions to ensure the highest quality of service. Our engineers and sales forces work together as a team, always ready to co-operate with clients to deliver on that promise.
